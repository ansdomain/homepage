Arbitrum Domains lets you register your own human-readable .Arb domain names that link, or "resolve", to addresses on the Arbitrum blockchain. Since this resolution system works for any data, and not just addresses, you can also link your name to avatars, social media links, IPFS content hashes, and even other names.
